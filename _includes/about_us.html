Adopt A Friend

All of our kitties are in loving foster homes. You can contact the foster family by visiting the Facebook page listed on each cat, or you can e-mail our application coordinator at Crystal.coan@yahoo.com.

All of our cats have been spayed/neutered, have the age-appropriate shots, and have been FeLeuk/FIV tested. The adoption fee of $50 will help cover some of these costs. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.

About Us

We are just some kitty-loving ladies (and a gentleman) who are doing our part to help cats who have somehow been, through no fault of their own, left behind. It is our goal to be a temporary, loving transition to their way to a forever home.
